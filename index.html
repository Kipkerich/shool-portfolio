About Wama Training Institute

Excellence in Medical Education

Wama Training Institute is a premier healthcare education institution dedicated to advancing medical knowledge and producing highly skilled healthcare professionals. Established with a vision to bridge the gap in healthcare education, we provide comprehensive training that combines theoretical knowledge with practical hands-on experience.

Our institution is committed to nurturing the next generation of healthcare providers who will make significant contributions to community health and wellbeing, both locally and internationally.

Courses Offered

Health Support Services

This program is designed to equip you with the vital knowledge and practical skills required for immediate, impactful contributions to patient care and healthcare efficiency. Through hands-on training and mentorship, you will master essential supportive functions, including infection control, hospital procedures, and effective patient interaction. By course completion, you will be prepared for entry-level roles as a healthcare assistant, patient attendant, or clinical support specialist, ready to thrive in the dynamic healthcare environment.
